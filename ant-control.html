Common Signs You Need Ant Control

Ant problems often begin before large numbers become visible. Common signs include:

  • Ant trails along floors, walls, and countertops
  • Ants around sinks, kitchens, and food storage areas
  • Small piles of soil near foundations or sidewalks
  • Winged ants appearing indoors
  • Recurring ant sightings after cleaning

Common Types of Ants Found in Coeur d'Alene

Several ant species may be found around homes and businesses in the area, including:

  • Sugar Ants
  • Pavement Ants
  • Odorous House Ants
  • Carpenter Ants

Each species behaves differently, making proper identification an important part of selecting an appropriate treatment.
