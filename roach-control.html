Common Signs You Need Cockroach Control

Cockroaches are nocturnal and excellent at hiding, so noticing them during the day may indicate a larger problem. Common signs include:

  • Night activity when kitchen lights toggle on
  • Small dark pepper-like droppings
  • Oily, musty scent in closed cabinets
  • Smear marks on walls or baseboards
  • Egg casings found in hidden, dark areas

Where Cockroaches Commonly Hide

Cockroaches prefer warm, dark, and moist environments. They are commonly found in:

Kitchen cabinets Under appliances Bathrooms Wall voids Utility rooms Pantries Drains and pipes Behind baseboards

Our Cockroach Control Process

Every property presents different conditions, so recommendations begin with understanding the problem.

1 Inspection

Identify cockroach species, nesting locations, entry points, and areas where cockroaches are commonly found.

2 Treatment Recommendations

Recommendations are based on the species involved, the property's layout, and the affected areas.

3 Targeted Treatment

Treatment focuses on common hiding places, utilizing baits, growth regulators, and precision material application.

4 Prevention Guidance

Recommendations may include sealing entry points, fixing moisture issues, and addressing sanitation factors that encourage roaches.

Why Professional Cockroach Control Matters

Cockroaches reproduce extremely fast and can hide in inaccessible areas. Treating only visible roaches rarely eliminates the source. Professional cockroach control uses targeted baits, insect growth regulators, and exclusion techniques to eliminate the entire population and help prevent future infestations.
